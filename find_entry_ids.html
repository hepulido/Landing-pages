How to Find Google Form Entry IDs

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. Go to your Google Form: Open Form
2. Right-click on the form and select "View Page Source" (or press F12)
3. In the source code, press Ctrl+F (or Cmd+F on Mac) and search for: entry.
4. Look for lines like: name="entry.1234567890"
5. Note down the entry IDs for each field:
  • Name field: entry._______
  • Business field: entry._______
  • Phone field: entry._______
  • Email field: entry._______
  • City field: entry._______
6. Once you have the entry IDs, I'll update the API route with the correct ones.

Alternative Method:

If you can't find the entry IDs in the source code, you can also:

  1. Fill out your form manually once
  2. Check the "Responses" tab in your Google Form
  3. Look at the column headers - they might show the entry IDs
